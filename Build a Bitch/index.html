Welcome to Disneyland you Evil Fiend

Create your own villain by frollicking around the park!

Go on rides to unlock spells, weapons, and tools to aid you in your villainous deeds! Repeating rides adds additional PP to each weapon or spell. Gain additional base HP everytime you eat and add one 10 HP health potion to your inventory everytime you have a fun little beverage. Gain XP for waiting in lines, interacting with characters, and taking in a show or parade.

XP Tracker

Earn 1 XP for every minute you wait in line, 10 XP for watching a show or parade, and 20 XP for interacting with characters. Level up and add 2 attack points to your base attack every 50 XP.

HP Tracker

A snack is worth 10 HP, a quick service meal is worth 15 HP, and a sit-down meal is worth 20 HP.
